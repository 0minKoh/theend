TheM Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 12, 2026

Welcome to TheM, a private on-device AI character messenger provided by TheEnd ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is deeply important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the TheM mobile application.

1. Ultimate Privacy & On-Device Processing

The foundational principle of TheM is absolute user privacy and data autonomy.

  • No Accounts: You can use TheM without registration or creating any account. We do not collect or store credentials, profiles, or personal identities.
  • No Conversation Data Collection: All chat histories, prompts, custom personas, and interactions are processed 100% locally on your device (On-Device). We do not run external conversational servers, and we have zero access to what you type or read within the app.

2. Information Collected Automatically (Analytics & Crash Reports)

While we do not collect your personal chats or account information, we use third-party tools to monitor application stability and improve user experience. These tools may automatically collect certain non-personally identifiable technical data.

The third-party service providers used by the app include:

Google Analytics for Firebase

Used to collect anonymous usage behavior statistics (e.g., app launches, screen views, feature usage frequency). This data helps us understand how the app is used without identifying individual users.

Firebase Crashlytics / CrashAnalytics

Used to collect diagnostic data regarding application crashes and performance issues (e.g., device type, operating system version, crash logs). This information is solely used to fix bugs and improve app stability.

3. How We Use the Technical Information

The collected anonymous technical data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To maintain, analyze, and optimize the performance and stability of the application.
  • To diagnose and debug software errors and critical system crashes.
  • To understand macro-level user trends to design better features.

4. Data Security

Since your essential data (conversations, custom personas) is stored strictly within your local device's secure sandbox environment, the security of that data relies heavily on your device's native security features. We recommend keeping your mobile operating system up-to-date and using secure device authentication.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our logs.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with a updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us via our official repository or communication channels.
